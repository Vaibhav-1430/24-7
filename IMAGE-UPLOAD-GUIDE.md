# üì∏ How to Add Pictures to Menu Items

## Quick Start Guide

### Method 1: Upload Your Own Images (Recommended)
1. **Go to Admin Portal** ‚Üí Menu Management
2. **Click "Add New Item"** or **Edit existing item**
3. **Click "Add Image"** button in the Image section
4. **Choose "Upload File" tab**
5. **Drag & drop** your image or **click "Choose File"**
6. **Preview** the image and **click "Use This Image"**

### Method 2: Use Free Online Images
1. **Click "Add Image"** ‚Üí **"Free Images" tab**
2. **Select from sample images** or **use "Image URL" tab**
3. **For custom images**: Visit free image sites:
   - [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/s/photos/food) (Free food photos)
   - [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/search/food/) (Free food photos)
   - [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/images/search/food/) (Free food photos)
4. **Right-click** on any image ‚Üí **"Copy image address"**
5. **Paste the URL** in the Image URL field

### Method 3: Use Your Own Server Images
1. **Upload images** to your `images/` folder via FTP/hosting panel
2. **Use relative path**: `images/your-image-name.jpg`
3. **Example**: `images/chicken-noodles.jpg`

## Image Guidelines

### ‚úÖ Best Practices
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Size**: Maximum 5MB per image
- **Dimensions**: 400x300px (4:3 ratio) works best
- **Quality**: High-resolution, well-lit food photos
- **Content**: Show the actual food item clearly

### üìê Recommended Dimensions
- **Menu Cards**: 400x300px (landscape)
- **Square Format**: 300x300px (works well too)
- **Minimum**: 200x150px
- **Maximum**: 1200x900px (will be resized automatically)

## Step-by-Step: Adding Images to Existing Items

### For Items Already in Menu:
1. **Login to Admin Portal**
2. **Go to Menu Management**
3. **Find your item** and **click Edit button** (pencil icon)
4. **Scroll to Image section**
5. **Click "Add Image"** button
6. **Choose your method** (Upload/URL/Gallery)
7. **Select/Upload your image**
8. **Click "Use This Image"**
9. **Save the item**

## Troubleshooting

### ‚ùå Image Not Showing?
- **Check URL**: Make sure the image URL is accessible
- **Check Format**: Only JPG, PNG, WebP are supported
- **Check Size**: Must be under 5MB
- **Try Different Source**: Some websites block direct linking

### üîß Common Issues
- **"Unable to load image"**: The URL might be broken or blocked
- **Image too large**: Resize to under 5MB
- **Slow loading**: Use compressed/optimized images

### üí° Pro Tips
- **Batch Upload**: Prepare all images first, then add them one by one
- **Consistent Style**: Use similar lighting/style for all food photos
- **Local Storage**: Upload to your server for fastest loading
- **Backup**: Keep copies of your images
